The Department of Statistical Sciences of the University of Padua is looking forward to organizing the next annual Workshop of the joint working group on "Adaptive Designs and Multiple Testing Procedures" of the German and Austro-Swiss Regions of the International Biometric Society.

Registration for the workshop is now open using the registration form.

We invite abstracts for contributed talks on the topics of Adaptive Designs and Multiple Testing. Deadline for submission is February 28, 2016. Abstracts may be submitted using the submission form.

To keep up to date with Working Group activities, follow us on Twitter or sign up for our newsletter.

We are looking forward to welcoming you in Padua.

Florian Klinglmueller, Livio Finos, Monica Chiogna (Local organizers)

Andreas Faldum (Working group chair)
